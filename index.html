Questions and boundaries

What Beacon is—and what it isn’t

Is this an AI therapist?

No. It provides reflection, tracking, coping tools, and recommendations that complement accountable human care.

Will my therapist see everything I enter?

No. You review and approve what is shared. Private reflections remain private unless you choose otherwise.

How is my information protected?

Beacon uses explicit consent, minimal data collection, clear retention controls, and simple export and deletion options aligned with European data-protection requirements.

Will it diagnose me from my mood or behaviour?

No. Beacon can identify possible patterns, but presents them as observations rather than diagnoses.

What happens if I need urgent help?

The service directs people to appropriate local emergency and crisis support. It is not a crisis service.
